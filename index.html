Welcome to DMG

We are real estate and natural resources investment company based in West Africa.

Real Estate Investment

Our real estate investments range from residential and commercial properties to large-scale mixed-use developments. With a keen eye for emerging markets and a deep understanding of local customs and regulations, we identify investment opportunities that offer strong potential for growth and value creation.

For Leasing

Our lease services include property evaluation, negotiation of lease terms, lease agreement drafting, and property management. We work with landlords to ensure that the lease agreement is tailored to your requirements and provides a fair and balanced agreement for both parties.

For Sale

Our sales services include property valuation, marketing and advertising, negotiation of sale terms, and assistance with legal documentation. We work with both buyers and sellers to ensure that the transaction is fair, transparent, and beneficial for both parties.

Natural Resources Investment

Our natural resources investments include gold, diamonds, cobalt, and other strategic minerals. We work with trusted partners and suppliers to ensure that our investments are ethically and sustainably sourced, and that they support local communities and economies.

About Us

At DMG, we believe that responsible investment is the key to unlocking Africa's vast potential. With over 30 years of experience in the real estate and natural resources sectors, our team has a proven track record of successfully developing, managing, and selling properties and resources across the continent.

Our approach is built on three core principles: Transparency, Collaboration, and Impact. We work closely with local communities, governments, and other stakeholders to ensure that our investments are socially and environmentally responsible, and that they create tangible benefits for everyone involved.

Our mission is to develop meaningful growth by investing in sustainable development projects that benefit local communities and create long-term value for our stakeholders.
